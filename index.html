Welcome to the Tic Tac Toe game!

Rules

Players take turns placing their symbols, usually "X" and "O," in empty cells. The objective is to be the first player to form a line of four of their symbols either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. The game ends when one player wins, or if all 16 cells are filled without a winner, resulting in a draw.

Choose player type

You can choose three types of game: Human vs. Human, Human vs. AI, AI vs. AI
