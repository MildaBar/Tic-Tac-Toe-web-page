Welcome to the Tic Tac Toe game!

Rules

  • Players take turns placing their symbols, "X" and "O," in empty cells.
  • Be the first player to form a line of four of their symbols either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
  • The game ends when one player wins, or if all 16 cells are filled without a winner, resulting in a tie.
  • FIRST OF ALL choose the game type and then press START GAME button.
